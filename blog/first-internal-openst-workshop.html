Our first internal Open-ST workshop!

Published on July 23, 2024 by N. Rajewsky Lab

openst workshop data science

0 min READ

We are excited to organize our first internal Open-ST workshop! Colleagues from both the wet and the dry lab came together to discuss and learn how to process Open-ST datasets. Our aim is to teach everyone interested in learning how to analyze this kind of data, regardless of the level of computational expertise.

Ultimately, we are going to offer Open-ST workshops outside of our lab. We are still thinking about the right format for this, but if you are interested in participating then feel free to reach out!
